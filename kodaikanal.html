Coaker's Walk

Coaker's Walk is a scenic pedestrian path offering spectacular views of the valley. It is perfect for a peaceful walk amidst nature.

Kodaikanal Lake

The man-made Kodaikanal Lake is an iconic spot for boating and relaxation. Surrounded by lush greenery, it's an ideal spot for nature lovers.

Bryant Park

One of the most beautiful parks in Kodaikanal, Bryant Park is known for its variety of flora, including exotic species of plants, flowers, and trees.

Pillar Rocks

Pillar Rocks is a breathtaking spot where three giant rock pillars stand tall against the backdrop of the beautiful valley. It's a must-see natural wonder in Kodaikanal.

Silver Cascade Waterfall

The Silver Cascade Waterfall is a beautiful cascade located on the Ghat road. The sight of the water gushing down the rocks makes it an excellent spot for photography and relaxation.
