3-Day Trip Itinerary - Ooty

Your ultimate 3-day getaway to Ooty, exploring the best of nature and heritage

Day 1: Arrival and Exploration

Morning:

  • Check-in: Arrive at your hotel (suggested check-in time: 12:00 PM). A few good options are The Destiny Farm Resort or Ooty Gate Hotel.
  • Breakfast: Enjoy a hearty South Indian breakfast at the hotel or nearby, like the Ooty’s famous Café Diem.

Afternoon:

  • Lunch: Head to a local restaurant for lunch, like Shinkows Restaurant, known for its great Indian and Chinese dishes.
  • Visit: Start your day’s exploration by visiting the iconic Ooty Lake. Enjoy a peaceful boat ride and take in the surrounding hills.

Evening:

  • Snack: Try some local snacks at a café like the Ooty Bakery and Café.
  • Visit: Head to the Botanical Garden to explore the wide variety of flora and relax amidst the beautiful landscape.

Night:

  • Dinner: Enjoy a traditional dinner at the Ooty Café or one of the many local eateries offering fresh and flavorful food.
  • Rest: Head back to your hotel to relax for the night.

Day 2: Adventure and Scenic Views

Morning:

  • Breakfast: Enjoy breakfast at the hotel or at the Café Royale.
  • Visit: Visit the famous Rose Garden, where you can see over 2,000 varieties of roses. It’s a great spot for photography!

Afternoon:

  • Lunch: Savor a delicious lunch at The Earl's Secret, known for its excellent ambiance and Continental food.
  • Visit: Explore Doddabetta Peak, the highest point in Ooty. Take in breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains.

Evening:

  • Snack: Refresh yourself with a hot cup of tea or coffee at the Ooty Tea Factory.
  • Visit: Afterward, take a relaxing ride on the Nilgiri Mountain Railway, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Night:

  • Dinner: Head back to your hotel and enjoy a leisurely dinner. You can opt for a local restaurant like The Place to Eat, known for delicious local dishes.
  • Rest: Return to your hotel and relax for the night.

Day 3: Nature and Departure

Morning:

  • Breakfast: Enjoy breakfast at the hotel or nearby café.
  • Visit: Explore the Thread Garden, a unique attraction where everything is made of thread. It’s a fascinating spot for art lovers.

Afternoon:

  • Lunch: Have a relaxing lunch at Pykara Lake Café while enjoying the picturesque surroundings.
  • Visit: After lunch, head to Pykara Lake for a peaceful boat ride amidst serene landscapes.

Evening:

  • Check-out: Check out of your hotel (suggested time: 11:00 AM).
  • Departure: Depart from Ooty, taking with you the wonderful memories of your trip.
