3-Day Trip Itinerary - Bangalore

Your perfect 3-day itinerary for an unforgettable trip to Bangalore

Day 1: Explore the City

Morning:

  • Check-in: Arrive at your hotel (suggested check-in time: 12:00 PM)
  • Breakfast: Have a traditional South Indian breakfast at MTR or Vidyarthi Bhavan.

Afternoon:

  • Lunch: Head to UB City for a meal at The 13th Floor, offering beautiful city views.
  • Visit: Lalbagh Botanical Garden for a relaxing afternoon walk in the gardens.

Evening:

  • Snack: Stop by for an evening snack at VV Puram Food Street.
  • Visit: Bangalore Palace for a historical exploration of royal architecture.

Night:

  • Dinner: Enjoy a delicious dinner at Toit Brewpub, one of Bangalore’s famous microbreweries.
  • Rest: Head back to your hotel for a peaceful night's rest.

Day 2: Nature and Shopping

Morning:

  • Breakfast: Start your day with breakfast at Saravana Bhavan for a traditional South Indian breakfast.
  • Visit: Spend the morning at Cubbon Park for a nature-filled experience.

Afternoon:

  • Lunch: Grab lunch at Koshy’s, a popular Bangalore spot for authentic South Indian and Continental food.
  • Visit: Explore Commercial Street for shopping, from trendy clothes to souvenirs.

Evening:

  • Snack: Try local street food or snacks from the food vendors near MG Road.
  • Visit: ISKCON Temple for a peaceful evening amidst its grand architecture.

Night:

  • Dinner: Have a rooftop dinner at Skyye, with a panoramic view of the city skyline.
  • Rest: Return to your hotel for the night.

Day 3: Wildlife & Relaxation

Morning:

  • Breakfast: Enjoy breakfast at the hotel or a local cafe.
  • Visit: Head to Bannerghatta National Park for a morning safari.

Afternoon:

  • Lunch: Have a hearty lunch at Karavalli, known for its coastal Karnataka cuisine.
  • Relax: Spend some time at the park or return to your hotel for a relaxed afternoon.

Evening:

  • Snack: Visit one of the cafes in the city like the famous Bangalore Cafe.
  • Visit: Stop by the Vidhana Soudha for an evening view of the grand building.

Night:

  • Dinner: Conclude your trip with a nice dinner at the Karnataka Food Hotel.
  • Check-out: Check-out from your hotel (suggested time: 11:00 AM). Safe travels!
