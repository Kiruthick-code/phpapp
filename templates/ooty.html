Ooty Lake

Ooty Lake is a popular tourist spot in Ooty. It offers a beautiful view of the surrounding hills and has boating facilities, making it a must-visit for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

Botanical Garden

The Botanical Garden in Ooty is one of the oldest and most famous gardens in India. Spread across 55 acres, it showcases a variety of flora, including many rare species.

Rose Garden

The Rose Garden in Ooty houses more than 2,000 varieties of roses. It is a peaceful retreat and offers panoramic views of the Nilgiri Hills, making it an ideal spot for photography and relaxation.

Doddabetta Peak

Doddabetta Peak is the highest point in the Nilgiri Mountains. It offers a spectacular view of Ooty and the surrounding hills, making it a favorite spot for trekking and sightseeing.

Nilgiri Mountain Railway

The Nilgiri Mountain Railway is a UNESCO World Heritage site and offers a scenic train journey through the hills. It is a charming experience, offering breathtaking views of Ooty's lush landscapes.

Thread Garden

The Thread Garden in Ooty is a unique garden where everything, including flowers, plants, and even animals, is made from thread. It's a creative and fascinating place to visit for art lovers.
