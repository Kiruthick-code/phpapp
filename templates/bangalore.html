Lalbagh Botanical Garden

Lalbagh is a 240-acre botanical garden known for its rich collection of tropical plants, a glasshouse modeled after London's Crystal Palace, and breathtaking landscapes.

Bangalore Palace

Bangalore Palace, with its Tudor-style architecture, is a magnificent structure that offers a glimpse into the regal history of the city.

Vidhana Soudha

This iconic government building is a beautiful example of Dravidian architecture and houses the Karnataka State Legislature. It is one of Bangalore's most recognized landmarks.

Cubbon Park

Located in the heart of the city, Cubbon Park is a lush green oasis offering a peaceful retreat amidst the urban hustle, perfect for walking, jogging, or simply relaxing.

ISKCON Temple

The ISKCON temple in Bangalore is one of the largest and most beautiful temples in India, offering a serene environment for spiritual seekers and visitors alike.

Bannerghatta National Park

This wildlife park, located on the outskirts of Bangalore, offers safaris, a zoo, and a butterfly park, making it a popular destination for nature lovers and families.

Commercial Street

For shopaholics, Commercial Street offers a variety of goods, from trendy clothes and accessories to souvenirs and spices. It's a bustling area full of life and energy.
