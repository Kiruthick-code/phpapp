3-Day Trip Itinerary - Kodaikanal

Your perfect 3-day itinerary for an unforgettable trip to Kodaikanal

Day 1: Scenic Exploration and Relaxation

Morning:

  • Check-in: Arrive at your hotel (suggested check-in time: 12:00 PM)
  • Breakfast: Enjoy a traditional South Indian breakfast at a local café, such as Tava or the Kodaikanal Bakery.

Afternoon:

  • Lunch: Have lunch at the amazing Hilltop Inn, which offers a beautiful view of the valley.
  • Visit: Start your exploration with Coaker's Walk. Enjoy the scenic view of the valley as you take a peaceful stroll.

Evening:

  • Snack: Try some local delicacies or grab a snack at the famous Café Cariappa.
  • Visit: Head to Kodaikanal Lake for a relaxing boat ride in the evening.

Night:

  • Dinner: Have dinner at the Royal Tibet restaurant, known for its delicious Tibetan cuisine.
  • Rest: Head back to your hotel for a good night's sleep.

Day 2: Nature and Adventure

Morning:

  • Breakfast: Enjoy breakfast at your hotel or a nearby café.
  • Visit: Visit Bryant Park to enjoy the tranquility and beauty of the park's wide variety of plants and flowers.

Afternoon:

  • Lunch: Enjoy a hearty lunch at the famous Cloud Street Café.
  • Visit: After lunch, visit Pillar Rocks for a mesmerizing view of the three giant rock formations that rise high above the surrounding valley.

Evening:

  • Snack: Stop by a local café for some hot tea and snacks to refresh yourself.
  • Visit: Visit Silver Cascade Waterfall in the late afternoon for a beautiful view of the water cascading down the rocks.

Night:

  • Dinner: Enjoy a cozy dinner at the Kodai Resort Hotel’s restaurant, which offers a mix of South Indian and international dishes.
  • Rest: Return to your hotel to relax for the night.

Day 3: Adventure and Departure

Morning:

  • Breakfast: Have a refreshing breakfast at your hotel.
  • Visit: Explore the scenic hills surrounding Kodaikanal by taking a short trek to the nearby hills or valleys.

Afternoon:

  • Lunch: Head to the Italian Café for some delightful pasta or pizza for lunch.
  • Relax: After lunch, you can visit the local market for some souvenir shopping or relax at a local café.

Evening:

  • Check-out: Check out of your hotel (suggested time: 11:00 AM).
  • Departure: Head to your next destination or return home with beautiful memories of Kodaikanal.
